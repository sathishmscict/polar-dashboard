Welcome!

This is Polar Dashboard, designed and developed by Aidan Follestad and Tom Wellington, with help from Daniel Ciao. This dashboard is designed to be different, fresh, and configurable. Try the sexiest and easiest to use dashboard. All that we ask is that you maintain credit to us when you publish your work.

You can download a sample of Polar from Google Play. The icon and Google Play banner for the sample were made by Anthony Nguyen, creator of Quantum Dots.

If you find any bugs, please report them via the GitHub issue tracker. If you are a coder, it would be even better if you could contribute!
